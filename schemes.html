💳 SmartCard

The SmartCard is a prepaid money system, just like a debit card. No need to carry paper every time you visit!

How it works:
First, pay the paper to Ishan. It is then stored safely in your personal account.

When you come to spend again in my store, you don't need to pay upfront. The cost gets cut directly from your Account balance.

Withdrawal: If you want to withdraw your paper back, please contact Ishan.

Fees: 1 paper is taken from your account every month as a maintenance fee.
